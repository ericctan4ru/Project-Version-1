About Our Organization

Dedicated to unity, service, and progress for every IT students.

Who We Are

NITE is an organization dedicated to empowering individuals through innovation, teamwork, and excellence. We strive to create opportunities for growth, promote collaboration, and inspire positive change within our community. Our commitment lies in nurturing talent, fostering creativity, and driving progress toward a brighter and more sustainable future.

Our History

Barangay San Rafael is one of the progressive barangays in our municipality. Known for its strong sense of community, the barangay has long been a center for cultural, religious, and social gatherings. Over the years, it has grown both in population and in services to better serve its residents.

Our Mission

To promote inclusivity, ensure public safety, support local development, and provide efficient services for every resident.

Our Vision

A united and progressive community where every resident thrives in a safe, supportive, and sustainable environment.
